About Us

History of Company

Inaugurated on August 2014, with a aim of venturing into entrepreneurial world and creating everything from the scratch, Innomind creations was born. Main aim of starting with Formal Shirts was it never ending demand in corporate Culture. Over the time company added to its work areas as well as exanded the product portfolio. Innomind creations was born with a aim to make mens fashion a daily wear accessory rather than luxury.

Rahul Tendulkar, Our Founder

He is a Founder in straddled the worlds of fashion and style, just as they were becoming distinguishable. One of the most enthusiast men of his age, he choose to start his startup rather than a cozy job. over the time he took his company from turnover of 9 lakhs to 1.03 cr in second year of being in business. Today he provides employment to over 38 ladies and 15 gents staff. Being a staunch supporter of women empowerment through employment, he provides majority of jobs to girls and women from ITI and skill India. He is connected with people on social as well as professional level that helps in creating a reliable workforce for sampling, designing and production.
